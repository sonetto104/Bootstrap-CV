About Me

What do I do?

I'm a skilled Full Stack Developer with a wealth of professional experience from my successful previous career in the classical music industry. I combine an ability to empathise with consumers with technical expertise on every project.

How do I do it?

I work effectively by identifying project requirements, itemising them into discrete categorised tasks and am able to communicate these effectively to a team working through an Agile development process.

Why hire me?

I'm very personable - being friendly and enthusiastic, I work well in teams and am excellent at maintaining positive relationships with clients.

I'm a good story builder - I understand that a technically beautiful project is worth nothing unless it satisfies well the needs of its imagined users. I don't just consider building user stories absolutely necessary; I even find it fun!

I have a keen eye for design - in today's competitive environment, I understand that if a project doesn't look good to the consumer, then it's not much good full stop. UX is key to the success of a project.

I'm open to criticism and want to learn - I'm happy to be critiqued at any time. I understand it's nothing personal, just about the success of the project!
