My groups and societies

Stamp Out The Stigma: Supporting Veterans' Mental Health

"Stamp Out The Stigma: Supporting Veterans' Mental Health" was the first hackathon I took part in this year organised by Code Institute, Trust in SODA and Ex-MilitaryCareers.com. It was a hugely enjoyable project for an important cause, and was great practice for working on an Agile development team.

Code Institute

I'm currently enrolled in Code Institute's Diploma in Full Stack Software Development Programme and am about 70% through the course. It has given me a great foundation in programming fundamentals using mainly JavaScript and Python, as well as great lessons in other dependencies like Bootstrap and Django. I'm really looking forward to using this qualification as the launch pad for my further learning.

Vesta Rowing Club

I joined Vesta Rowing Club in September of last year and am now the Social Secretary for the Development Squad. I absolutely love the camaraderie we have in our squad and the intensity we have in our training. I'm so excited to be preparing for the Royal Henley qualifiers in June!

Music

Though I'm now making the career leap into tech, music was my primary focus for most of my life. It's been an incredible journey playing solo and chamber music recitals all over Europe and North America, concerto solos in Ireland and Italy, being a member of the European Union Youth Orchestra in 2022 and playing all sorts of other gigs in between. I also take great pride in my students who range from 4 years old to middle age and all play with enthusiasm and quality. While I may be saying goodbye to music as a job, I still love to practise and to play with friends.
